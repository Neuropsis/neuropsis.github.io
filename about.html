About Neuropsis

Neuropsis is a digital space curated by physicians and neuroscientists to make clinical neuroimaging, translational neuroscience, and neuromodulation accessible to a wider audience. This site bridges medicine and research through educational content, case reviews, and integrated tools.

We believe that medical knowledge—especially in the neurosciences—should be clear, visual, and practically useful. Our posts draw from current research, imaging cases, and hands-on clinical experience. Whether you're a student, clinician, or researcher, Neuropsis is built to support your curiosity and critical thinking.

Content includes fMRI, EEG, TMS, and emerging neurotechnologies. Each article is written with a focus on clarity, context, and clinical or experimental relevance.
